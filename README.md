**BEU Online Boutique**

Welcome to BEU Online Boutique – an elegant and modern e-commerce platform designed to Illuminate The Beauty Within You. This project provides a seamless online shopping experience with a focus on style, usability, and efficiency.

** Features**

Modern UI/UX – A clean and intuitive interface for easy navigation.

Responsive Design – Optimized for desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Product Management – Easy-to-manage inventory with categories and filtering.

Shopping Cart & Checkout – Smooth purchasing process with multiple payment options.
